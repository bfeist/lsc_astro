# **Introduction**

Station 8 was supposed to be on a ray from South Ray Crater. Soil sample 68120-68124 was collected from a "fillet" beside a large boulder (figure 1), directly beneath a "spall zone" on the side of the boulder (Horz et al. 1972). A double drive tube was collected nearby.

## **Petrography**

The maturity index of 68121 is Is /FeO = 61 (Morris 1978). The mineral mode, agglutinate count and grain size distribution have not been determined for this soil.

## **Chemistry**

Compston et al. (1973) determined the major element content (figure 4), while Fruchter et al. (1974) determined the race element content (figure 6).

Moore et al. (1973) determined 170 ppm carbon for 68121 (figure 5). Kerridge et al. (1975) determined 126 ppm carbon and 83 ppm nitrogen, and Moore and Lewis (1975) reported 83 ppm and 117 ppm nitrogen for 68121, respectively.

Cirlin and Housley (1981) determined 54 - 80 ppb Cd and 22.3 ppm Zn.

#### **Other Studies**

Bhandari et al. (1973) determined the "suntan" exposure age of 68121 by measurement of the density of fossil nuclear tracks.
