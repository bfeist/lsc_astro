## **Introduction**

67627 and 67629 are glass-cemented aggregates that were collected as rake samples from the regolith just inside the rim of North Ray Crater – see section on 67601 and figure 6. They include numerous white feldspathic particles. The exposure age of these glass particles is about that of NRC.

# **Radiogenic age dating**

Borchardt et al. (1986) reported Ar/Ar ages of 1.19 b.y. for 67627 and 1.41 b.y. for 67629. A clast in 67627 was 3.63 b.y. old (figure 9).

## **Cosmogenic isotopes and exposure ages**

Borchardt et al. (1986) determined the 38Ar exposure age of 67627 to be 40 or 44 m.y., and 43 m.y. for 67629. This about the age of NRC.
