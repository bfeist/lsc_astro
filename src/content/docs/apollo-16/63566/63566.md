# **Introduction**

63566 and 63575 may be parts of the series 63559 – 63576. They are all rake samples with similar appearance from station 13, on the flank of North Ray Crater. These two small glass samples have relatively large white inclusions (figure 1 and 2).

# **Petrography**

Warner et al. (1973) describe 63566 as "dendritic to spherulitic devitrified glass" and 63575 as "glass-cementing light clasts". Borchardt et al. (1986) discuss these fragments in terms of "glass bombs". The glass is devitrified where it is in contact with rock.
