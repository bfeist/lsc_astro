## **Introduction**

67628 was renumbered by Ryder and Norman (1980) when they made the catalog. It is a collection of four "glass bombs" that were collected as rake samples from just inside the rim of North Ray Crater – see section on 67601.

## **Petrography**

Borchardt et al. (1985) correctly labeled these particles "glass bombs". They include a collection of lithic fragments as clasts (figures 1 – 4). They are not unlike the collection that is 67629.

## **Chemistry**

None

# **Radiogenic age dating**

Not

## **Processing**

67628 was originally cataloged as 4 glass particles, but Ryder and Norman (1980) treated them as separate particles – so they were renumbered. They each have a thin section.
