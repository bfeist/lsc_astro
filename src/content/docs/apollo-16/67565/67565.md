## **Introduction**

67565 and 67668 are rake samples from the south rim of North Ray Crater – see sections on 67481 and 67601. They have similar micropoikitiic texture with interlocking oikocrysts of pyroxene surrounding chadocrysts of plagioclase. Ilmenite is concentrated at the boundaries of the pyroxene oikocysts. They also have similar composition.

## **Chemistry**

Stoffler et al. (1985) reported the bulk composition based on defocused beam analysis of thin sections. Wanke received splits for analysis.
