# **63578**

## Feldspathic Fragmental Breccia 19.6 grams

![](_page_0_Picture_2.jpeg)

*Figure 1: Photo of 63578 after chipping. Ruler is in cm/mm. S72-55400.* 

#### **Introduction**

63578 is a rake sample from station 13, on the flank of North Ray Crater – see section on 63500. It is a fragmental breccia with at least some glass in the matrix.

#### **Petrography**

Warner et al. (1973) termed 63578 a "*slightly metamorphosed glassy breccia*". Korotev (1996) dubbed it "*feldspathic fragmental breccia*". There appears to be a lot of glass in the matrix (figure 2).

#### **Chemistry**

The composition of 63578 is reported in Korotev (1996). One wonders what Rhodes did with his split!

#### **Radiogenic age dating**

None
