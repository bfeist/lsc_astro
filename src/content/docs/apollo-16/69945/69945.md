## **69945** Micropoikititic Impact Melt 6.88 grams

![](_page_0_Picture_1.jpeg)

*Figure 1: Photo of 69945. Scale in cm. S72-40137.* 

## **Introduction**

69945 is a fine-grained, poikilitic impact melt collected from the soil adjacent to boulder 69935/55. It appears to be more mafic than most poikilitic Apollo 16 breccias and has only minor clasts (figure 2).

Norman et al. (2006) determined that 69945 was 3.877 ± 0.011 b.y. old (figure 4).

## **Processing**

69945 was sawn to produce an end piece which broke (figure 3). There are 5 thin sections of 69945.
