## **Chemistry**

The composition of some of these sample was determined by broad beam electron probe analysis by McKinley et al. (1983). The trace elements were determined for 64546 (table 1) and are quite high. Presumably this is for the dark material, because the light material is almost all plagioclase. Note the very high Ni, Ir and Au content. Thus the dark material is an impact melt.
