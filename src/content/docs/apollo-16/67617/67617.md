#### **Introduction**

67617 is a moderately coherent impact melt breccia that was collected as a rake sample from the rim of North Ray Crater (NRC) – see section on 67601 (figures 1 and 2). It has zap pits on one surface.

## **Petrography**

67617 has abundant plagioclase clasts held in a recrystallized, now-poikilitic matrix (Ryder and Norman 1980). Steele and Smith (1973) analyzed the pyroxene (figure 4) and the plagioclase An90-97.

#### **Chemistry**

None reported
