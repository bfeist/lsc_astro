## **Introduction**

68845 and its companions were sieved from a large soil collected at station 8, Apollo 16 – see section on 68840. They are coherent, grey, aphanitic rocks. Thin sections are not available at this time.

## **Chemistry**

The composition of these particles is reported as and average (Korotev 1994). They appear to be very feldspathic (table).
