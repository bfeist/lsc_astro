# **67626**

## Impact melt Breccia 19.2 grams

![](_page_0_Picture_2.jpeg)

*Figure 1: Photo of 67626. Sample is about 4 cm long. S72-51267* 

### **Introduction**

67626 is one of many rake samples collected from the inside rim of North Ray Crater, Apollo 16 - see section on 67600. It is an ugly piece of glass.

### **Petrography**

Stoffler et al. (1985) listed 67626 as a "partly devitrified glass bomb". It has not been studied.
