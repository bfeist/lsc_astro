## **63596** Vesicular Poikilitic Impact Melt 6.4 grams

![](_page_0_Picture_1.jpeg)

*Figure 1: Phot of 63596. Sample is 2 cm across. NASA S80-37419.* 

63596 is a rake sample with a fine-grained poikilitic texture where pyroxene oikocrysts surround laths of plagioclase. It is clastic and vesicular (figure 2).

Norman et al. (2006) used the Ar/Ar plateau technique to date 63596 (figure 3).

There are 3 thin sections.
