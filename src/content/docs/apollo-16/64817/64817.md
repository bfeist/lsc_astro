# **64817**

## Basaltic Impact Melt 9 grams

![](_page_0_Picture_2.jpeg)

*Figure 1: Photo of 64817. S72-55334 33 mm long* 

### **Introduction**

64817 is a rake sample collected from the rim of a small crater at station 4 on Stone Mountain – see section on 64801. It is an aluminous basalt with prominent plagioclase laths and an age of 3.84 b.y.

### **Petrography**

64817 has a subophitic basaltic texture (figure 2). Warner et al. (1973) reported the composition of pyroxene (figure 3).

### **Chemistry**

Allocated to Clive Neal

### **Radiogenic age dating**

Norman et al. (2006) determined an age of 3.84 ± 0.02 b.y. for 64817 by the Ar/Ar plateau technique (figure 4).
