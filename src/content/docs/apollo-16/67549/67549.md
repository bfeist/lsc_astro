### **Introduction**

67549 and 67546 are rake samples from the rim of North Ray Crater – see section on 67481. They are porous and rounded with a light matrix and both light and dark clasts.

### **Petrography**

67549 has about 35 % low-Ca pyroxene; the rest is plagioclase (Ryder and Norman 1980). It also has some clasts with light-brown melt (devitrifed?)(figure 4).

### **Chemistry**

Stoffler et al. (1985) have reported the bulk composition (table) and referred to the samples as "microporphyritic melt breccias".
