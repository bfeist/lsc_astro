## **Introduction**

67529 was collected by rake from the soil near the White Boulders on the rim of North Ray Crater - see section on 67481. It is a cataclastic anorthosite, not unlike the boulder (67455).

## **Petrography**

Figure 3 shows that the plagioclase in 67429 is highly shocked, sheared and even melted. Hanson et al. (1979) and Steele et al. (1980) reported analyses of the plagioclase (An97) and pyroxene (En50)(figure 4).
