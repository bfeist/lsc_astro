# 67948

# Highland Basalt

1.59 grams

![](_page_0_Picture_3.jpeg)

Figure 1: Photo of 67948. Sample is 1.3 cm long. S80-40831

#### Introduction

67948 was sieved from the soil collected next to Outhouse Rock – see section on 67941. It is a plagioclase-rich, ophitic basalt with igneous texture (figure 2). It is highly aluminous and has high potassium content.

## Chemistry

Although the largest piece of this important sample was allocated, no analysis has been forthcoming.
