## **65056**

# Glass Matrix Breccia

64.8 grams

## **Introduction**

65056 is a large glass object that contains interesting white clasts (figures 1 and 3).

## **Petrography**

The glass in 65056 has devitrified as various variolitic textures (bow-ties, sheaths and radiating clusters of plagioclase). The white clasts are about 1 cm in dimention.

One white clast has large plagioclase crystals, and would be called an anorthosite.

Another white clast has a poikioblastic texture (figure ) and may be a norite.

## **Chemistry**

Morris et al. (1986) and See et al. (1986) studied 65056.

## **Cosmogenic isotopes and exposure ages**

Rancitelli et al. (19730 determined the 22Na and 26Al activity due to cosmic ray activation.
