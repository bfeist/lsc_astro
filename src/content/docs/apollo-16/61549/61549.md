# **61549** Mafic Impact Melt

3.76 grams

![](_page_0_Picture_2.jpeg)

*Figure 1: Photo of 61549. Tick marks are 1 mm. S72-55347* 

## **Introduction**

61549 was collected as a rake sample near Plum Crater – see section on 61500. It is a coherent dense impact melt with microlites of mafic minerals in aphanitic matrix. It is similar to 63506.

## **Petrography**

Warner et al. (1973) termed 61549 *"metabasalt with meta-norite clast". "The texture of this rock reflects the metamorphism. The matrix of the rock consists of prismatic to skeletal olivine crystals (Fo83) and Hshaped, skeletal plagioclase tablets set in a groundmass of anhedral pyroxene and fine-grained plagioclase (figure ). Immersed in this matrix are abundant plagioclase relics, few pink spinel relics and one large meta-norite."* 

The composition of mafic minerals in 61549 are given by Warner et al. (figure 2).
