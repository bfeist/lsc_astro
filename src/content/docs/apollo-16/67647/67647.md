#### **Introduction**

67647 was collected as a rake sample from just inside the rim of North Ray Crater (NRC) – see section on 67601. It is relatively coherent with lots of zap pits (figure 1).

#### **Petrography**

67647 appears to be a highland regolith breccia with a low maturity index Is/FeO = 2 (Jerde et al. 1987). However, it is broadly similar to other crushed white rocks on the rim of NRC. It has glass clasts, and glass in the matrix (figure 3).

#### **Chemistry**

Jerde et al. (1987) found that 67647 was highly aluminous, with low trace element content, but relatively high Ir.
