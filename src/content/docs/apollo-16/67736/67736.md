# **67736** Crystalline Impact Breccia

![](_page_0_Picture_1.jpeg)

*Figure 1: Photo of 67736 with pink spinel. Scale bar in mm. S80-28529* 

**Introduction Processing** 67736 is a rake sample collected from the rim of North There is only one thin section. Ray Crater – see section on 67701. It has zap pits (figure3).

## **Petrography**

Stoffler et al. (1981) describe the matrix as "micropoikilitic". Photos of the exterior show what appears to be pink spinel (?) grains (not confirmed).

## **Chemistry**

None

## **Radiogenic age dating**

Not
