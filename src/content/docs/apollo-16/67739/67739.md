### **Introduction**

67739 is a rake sample collected from the rim of North Ray Crater – see section on 67701. It is a breccia with glass matrix and rare poikiloblastic impactite fragments (figure 2).

#### **Petrography**

67739 was studied by Steele and Smith (1973) who reported mineral analyses (figure 3). It has a brown color, probably due to glass. It has abundant plagioclase (An ) and plagioclase-rich clasts. Mafic minerals 98-90 and opaques are rare.

#### **Chemistry**

None
