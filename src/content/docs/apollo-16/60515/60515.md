## **Introduction**

60515 thru 60519 are small rake samples from the LM site at Apollo 16. They all look alike, but could be different samples, because the surfaces are mostly coated with micrometeorite craters (figures 1 and 2). All are cataclastic anorthosite – of the ferroan variety.

## **Petrography**

Warren et al. (1983) gave a brief description and an analysis of 60515. Dowty et al. (1974) and Warner et al. (1976) gave a similar description of 60516. They contain about 98 % plagioclase (An97) and 1-2 % pyroxene (figure 3). Bersch et al. (1991) determined the exact composition of pyroxene. 60519 has dark inclusions, yet to be studied.

## **Chemistry**

Warren et al. (1983) reported an analysis of 60615, and Dowty et al. (1974) determined major elements for 60516 (table).
