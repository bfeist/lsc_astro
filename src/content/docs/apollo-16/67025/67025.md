#### Introduction

67025 was collected from the regolith near 67016 on the rim of North Ray Crater. It is a glass-coated crystalline rock with basaltic melt surrounding xenoliths of plagioclase.

# **Petrography**

Warner et al. (1973) termed 67025 a basalt, while Ryder and Norman (1980) termed it a basaltic impact melt. Plagioclase laths 100 microns long are surrounded and enclosed by pyroxene (figure 3). Numerous relict xenoliths of plagioclase indicated the precursor was a breccia.

Mineral compositions have not been determined.

#### **Chemistry**

The sample has high  ${\rm Al_2O_3}$ , with high Mg/Fe ratio. Ni is high

# Radiogenic age dating

None

# **Processing**

There are two thin sections, both from the same potted butt.
