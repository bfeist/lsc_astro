Brown Soil Breccia

## **Introduction**

65515 to 65574 are a collection of very friable, rounded, brown-colored soil breccias – see section on 65501. These classic soil clods have never been studied. There is a brief report of a poikilitic clast in 65575 in Warner et al. (1976). 65585 – 65587 are a collection of large agglutinates (glass splashes).
