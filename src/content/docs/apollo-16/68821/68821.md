#### **Introduction**

Soil sample 68821 was collected adjacent to a small boulder (figure 1). It should be compared with 68841, collected nearby.

### **Petrography**

Soil sample 68821 is a mature soil with maturity index Is /FeO = 84. According to the soil catalog (Morris et al. 1983), the average grain size is 112 microns and the agglutinate count is 52%.

Marvin (1972) cataloged the 4 – 10 mm coarse fines and Simkin et al. (1973) studied the mineralogy of 68821.

#### **Chemistry**

Fruchter et al. (1973) and Simkin et al. (1973) reported analyses of 68821. It has the same composition as other samples on the Cayley Plain.

Moore et al. (1973) determined 200 ppm carbon for 68821 (figure 5). Moore and Lewis (1975) reported 110 ppm nitrogen (these are probably saturated values).
