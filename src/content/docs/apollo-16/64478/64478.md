### **Introduction**

64478 is part of the residue of doc bag #3 which included 64475 and 64476.

## **Petrography**

Ryder and Norman (1980) term 64478 a "glass-coated impact melt". The interior has a poikilitic texture in places (figure 2).

### **Chemistry**

Korotev (1994) appears to have analyzed 64478, calling it "2F".

### **Processing**

There are 2 thin sections of 64478.
