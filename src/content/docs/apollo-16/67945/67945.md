### **Introduction**

Samples larger than 1 cm were extracted from soil samples and individually numbered – see section on soil 67941. This soil was from the crack between House Rock and Outhouse Rock (Sutton et al. 1981). One might expect that this fragment is from the big breccia, but there is no way to confirm that.

## **Petrography**

Stoffler et al. (1985) classified this rock as micropoikilitic impact breccia (figure 3), but gave no analysis. Ryder and Norman (1980) give the only other description.

#### **Radiogenic age dating**

Not
