## **14071** Olivine Basalt 2.16 grams

![](_page_0_Picture_1.jpeg)

*Figure 1: Photo of 14071. Sample is 2 cm across. S71-26072.* 

## **Introduction**

Sample 14071 was picked up on the flank of Cone Crater (Swann et al. 1971). It has not been allocated.

## **Petrography**

14071 has homogeneous igneous texture (Wilshire and Jackson 1972; Carlson and Walton 1978). The only thin section (,2) shows a high proportion of mafic minerals. (olivine and high-Ca pyroxene)(figure 2). It is probably a mare basalt.
