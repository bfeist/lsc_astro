## **Introduction**

14309 is a grab sample, collected somewhere along the traverse to Cone Crater (location unknown). It is a rounded vitric matrix breccia and has not been studied. There are glass-lined impact pits on the surface (figure 1).

## **Petrography**

Thin sections show the matrix of 14309 is cemented by vesicular glass (figure 2). Chao et al. (19720 and Simonds et al. (1977) would consider this a glass-matrix regolith breccia.

Vaniman et al. (1981) found the glass had the composition of medium KREEP.

## **Chemistry**

None

## **Processing**

Sample 14309 was returned in weigh bag 1031 with other grab samples from EVA2.
