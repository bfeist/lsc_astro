#### **Introduction**

14274 is one of the samples collected as part of the comprehensive sample (figure 2). It has a rounded surface with zap pits (figure 1). It is a coherent microbreccia with crystalline matrix. It has not been dated.

#### **Petrography**

Phinney et al. (1975) and Simonds et al. (1977) classify 14274 as a crystalline-matrix breccia. It has a seriate grain size distribution and most clasts are individual minerals (figure 3).

#### **Chemistry**

None
