### **Mineralogy**

*Olivine:* Olivine in 12035 is Fe-rich (Fo64) although the first olivine to crystallize should have been Mgrich (Fo80+), indicating that olivine in 12035 reequilibrated sub-solidus. Butler (1973) determined the minor element content of olivine (figure 6).

*Pyroxene:* Schnetzler and Phillpotts (1971) studied the distribution of trace elements between pyroxene and plagioclase in 12035.

*Plagioclase:* Walker et al. (1976) found that 12035 had the largest grains of plagioclase (~1 mm) of the Apollo 12 olivine basalts.
