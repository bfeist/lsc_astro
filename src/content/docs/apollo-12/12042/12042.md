### **Introduction**

12042 are fines collected in documented bag 12. They were from the outer flank of Surveyor Crater (figure 2).

### **Petrography**

The maturity index for 12042 is Is /FeO = 61 (Morris 1987). Frondel et al. (1971) determined the mineral mode, but did not specify agglutinates. The average grain size is 58 or 95 microns, depending on who sieved the sample (figure 5 a,b).

Marvin (1978) cataloged the coarse particles. Simon and Papike (1985) describe one particles from 12042.
