*Olivine:* Olivine composition is Fo78-67 (Champness et al. 1971).

## **Radiogenic age dating**

Not dated.

## **Other Studies**

Bogard et al. (1971) and Funkhouser et al. (1971) reported the content and isotopic composition of rare gases in 12075. Wrigley (1971) determined the cosmicray-induced activity of 22Na (74 dpm/kg) and 26Al (107 dpm/kg).
