# **12012** Olivine Basalt 176.2 grams

![](_page_0_Picture_1.jpeg)

*Figure 1: Photo of 12012,0, taken in 1991, after subdivision. NASA # S91-38939.* 

## **Introduction**

Olivine basalt 12012 has not been well studied and no age is available.

## **Petrography**

Rhodes et al. (1977) describe the texture of 12012 as "porphyritic-subophitic" with partially resorbed olivine phenocrysts in a subophitic groundmass (figure 2).

## **Mineralogy**

*Olivine:* Average Fo67 (Baldridge et al. 1979).

*Plagioclase:* Average An87.

*Chromite-Ulvöspinel:* Figure 4 shows chrome spinel overgrown by ulvöspinel, with metallic iron attached.

## **Chemistry**

The chemical composition of 12012 was determined by LSPET (1970), Rhodes et al. (1977) and Neal et al. (1994).
