# **Introduction**

12029 is the soil recovered from the Surveyor (III) scoop. It has been found to be similar to lunar soil 12070 (Dwornik et al. 1974).

Various reports on the analysis of the Surveyor parts are to be found at the end of the Second Lunar Science Proceedings.

# **Petrography**

The maturity of 12029 has not been determined. Dwornik et al. found 12029 to be entirely typical of Apollo 12 soil.

# **Chemistry**

Dwornik et al. (1974) gives the only analysis of 12029.
