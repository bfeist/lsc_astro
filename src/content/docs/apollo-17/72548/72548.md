## **Introduction**

72548 and 72549 are rake samples from the South Massif (figure 2). They are described as microgranular impact melt breccias by Warner et al. (1978).

# **Petrography**

72548 and 72549 appear to have similar modes, mineral compositions (figures 8 and 9) and textures. They are clastic in nature but have been partially melted and recrystallized (figures 4 - 7). The crystalline matrix appears different from most impact melt rocks, but that may be unimportant.
