## **Introduction**

71596 and 71537 are olivine-microporphyritic ilmenite basalt similar to 71569 and 71586 (Warner 1978).

71525 - 71596 etc. are rake samples collected as part of a comprehensive sample at station 1, taken near Steno Crater, Apollo 17. They include numerous small ilmenite basalts.

![](_page_1_Figure_0.jpeg)

*Figure 3: Composition of pyroxene and olivine in 71596 and 71537 (Warner et al. 1978).* 

## **Petrography**

71596 and 71537 have small phenocrysts of equant olivine and acicular ilmenite set in a variolitic groundmass (figure 4 and 5). Sheaths of plagioclase and pyroxene are intergrown in bowties. Olivine includes Cr-spinel grains. Armalcolite is present (Warner et al. 1976).

## **Chemistry**

Warner et al. (1975) reported and analysis of 71596. The composition of 71537 is given in the section on 71586.

## **Processing**

There is only one thin section of 71596.
