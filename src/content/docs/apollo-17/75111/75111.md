### **Chemistry**

Korotev and Kremser (1992) determined high FeO and Sc content (figure 3).

Gibson and Moore (1974) reported 1260 ppm sulfur and Gibson and Andrawes (1978) studied nitrogen release by crushing soil (117 ppm N), but somehow the carbon content was not determined.
