# **76221**

Soil 613 grams

*Figure 1: Map of station 6, Apollo 17.* 

## **Introduction**

76220 was collected from the boulder track above the large station 6 boulder at the base of the North massif. This soil was from the highest point reached on the North massif.

### **Petrography**

Morris (1978) determined the maturity index (Is /FeO =66).

Meyer (1973) reported 6 soil breccias, 4 agglutinates, 2 anorthosites, 5 feldspathic basalts, 5 feldspathic breccias – but no mare basalt particles in the 4 – 10 mm coarse fines.
