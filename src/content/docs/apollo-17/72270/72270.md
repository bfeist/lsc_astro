# **72210, 72230, 72250 and 72270**

bag residue 1.8. 1.7, 7.7 and 26.1 grams

### **Introduction**

These samples are fines from the bags that returned 72215, 72235, 72255 and 72275 – all from boulder #1, station 2, Apollo 17. See also section on soil samples 72221 etc. from base of boulder. In addition there is a large amount of fines generated by processing these rock samples.

## **Petrography**

These samples are only important in that they contain numerous zircons.

### **Chemistry**

None
