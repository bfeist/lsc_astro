## **Introduction**

Sample 73130 was collected as a "soil clod" from the bottom of a small, fresh crater. The astronauts referred to it as "instant rock", and sure enough it completely broke up during transit.

## **Petrography**

Morris (1978) reported the maturity index (Is/FeO = 16), which is way immature ! The sample has very low FeO (Korotev and Kremser (1992).
