# **Introduction**

76565 and 76566 were collected as rake samples at station 6 on the lower slope of the North Massif. They are fairly coherent regolith breccias, with numerous small clasts of feldspathic material from the highlands and some clasts of mare material from the valley floor.

# **Petrography**

Fruland (1983) and Simon et al. (1990) included 76565 in their study of regolith breccias. The dark matrix is non-porous and is mostly glass, with a few percent mare and highland clasts. . Orange glass is present.

#### *Significant Clast*

Warren et al. (1983) studied the white clast, finding that it was non-pristine or badly contaminated by Ir and Au.
