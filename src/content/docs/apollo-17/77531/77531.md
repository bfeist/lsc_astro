#### Introduction

77531 and 77511 are rake samples from station 7 on the flank of the North Massif (Apollo 17) (figure 1 and 2).

# **Petrography**

The maturity of 77531 and 77511 is  $I_s/FeO = 79$  and 80, respectively. The agglutinate content is 54 %
