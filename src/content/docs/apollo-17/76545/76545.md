# **Introduction**

76545 – 76549 are from a rake sample taken at station **Processing** 6 (figure ). They look alike and were grouped together Only 76545 and 76548 have thin sections. in the initial exam by Phinney et al. (1974).

# **Petrography**

Phinney et al. (1977) used the SEM to study the matrix of 76545.

Lots of small white clasts set in black glass matrix. Some particles also have shiny black glass (Meyer 1994).

# **Chemistry**

Only 76545 has been analyzed (table 1) and it is exactly the same as the soil collected with it.
