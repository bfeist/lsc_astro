# **74246**

# Unusual Breccia 28.8 grams

![](_page_0_Picture_2.jpeg)

*Figure 1: Photo of 74246. Scale in cm.* 

## **Introduction**

74246 was found as a large particle in the trench samples from the rim of Shorty Crater – see section on 74220.

## **Petrography**

Fruland (1983) and Simon et al. (1990) included 74246 in their study of regolith breccias, although it is not made up of regolith components. It has an abundance of red glass with very high Ti content (TiO2 = 14%). It also has fragments of mare basalt and minerals from mare basalt (figure 2).

## **Chemistry**

The composition of 74246 is very Ti- and Fe-rich (figure 3).
