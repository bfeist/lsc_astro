## **Introduction** <sup>25</sup>

76120- 76124 is a soil sample collected at LRV – 9 on the smooth mare regolith surface. It did not include large particles and has high maturity. <sup>15</sup>

### **Petrography** <sup>10</sup>

Morris (1978) determined the maturity index (Is /FeO =71). <sup>5</sup>
