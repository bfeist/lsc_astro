# 72150

Soil (bag residue) 53.3 grams

![](_page_0_Figure_2.jpeg)

Figure 1: Location of soil sample 72150 at LRV-3 on Apollo 17 map (Meyer 1973). S73-24071

#### Introduction

72150 was collected at LRV - 3 along with basalt sample 72155 (figure 1). Although this is a "bag residue", the high maturity indicates it is a proper soil (it is not easy to pick up a rock from the regolith surface without getting some soil).

# **Petrography**

The maturity index of 72150 is  $I_s/FeO = 82$  and the average grain size is 50 microns (Morris 1978, Graf

1993). Heiken and McKay (1974) determined the modal content, finding 53% agglutinate.

#### Chemistry

The composition of 72150 is intermediate between mare and highland material (figure 2 and 3). It is identical to soil sample 72161 collected at the same location.
