### **Introduction**

Lunar soil sample 79120 was collected adjacent to the boulder where 79135 was collected (figure 1). The location is on the rim of Van Serg Crater (figure 2). The soil from the rake sample, 79510, collected nearby, has the same chemical composition.

### **Petrography**

The maturity index of 79121 is Is /FeO = 57 (Morris 1978). A small breccia sample, 79125, was found during sieving. The coarse-fine particles, 79124, were documented by (Meyer 1973) and are mostly Van Serg Breccia like 79135.
