## **76330**

## Soil (with crumbs)? 306 grams

## **Introduction**

76330 is the fine material returned in the bag with 76335 (a very friable troctolitic anorthosite). After initial cataloging (Butler 1973), several additional pieces were picked out of 76330 and added to 76335. Most of 76330 has been sieved, but no measurements are reported.
