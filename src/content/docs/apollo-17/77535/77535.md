### Radiogenic age dating

Nyquist et al. (1976) determined Rb, Sr and Sr<sup>87/86</sup>. Although 77535 is coarse-grained, and mineral separation should be easy, they did not determined an internal mineral isochron. The zirconolite in these samples, contains high U (table 3) and should prove easy to date by U-Pb ion probe.

## **Processing**

77535 and 77536 have not been subdivided, nor much allocated. They only have three small thin sections between them.
