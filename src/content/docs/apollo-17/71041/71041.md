## **Chemistry**

The Ti content of these soil samples is high (9.5%) and they have a REE pattern similar to that of the A17 mare basalts (figure 5).

LSPET (1973) and Moore et al. (1974) reported 90 and 40 ppm carbon for 71041 and 71061, respectively (figure 4).

## **Cosmogenic isotopes and exposure ages**

Rancitelli et al. (1974) determined the cosmic-rayinduced activity of 22Na = 123 dpm/kg, 26Al = 123 dpm/kg, 46Sc = 75 dpm/kg, 48V = 27 dpm/kg, 54Mn = 198 dpm/kg and 56Co = 379 dpm/kg.
