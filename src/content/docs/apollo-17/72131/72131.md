## Introduction

Soil sample 72131 was collected from the mare surface at LRV-1 (figure 1) along with rock sample 72135.

# **Petrography**

Morris (1978) determined the maturity index (I<sub>s</sub>/FeO =60).

The large sample in the same bag, 72135, is an unusual breccia composed of mare basalt (Neal and Taylor 1992).

# **Chemistry**

The chemical composition of 72131 is similar to the ALSEP site.
