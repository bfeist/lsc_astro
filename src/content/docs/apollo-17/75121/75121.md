# **75121** Soil 375 grams

![](_page_0_Figure_1.jpeg)

*Figure 1: Location of soil sample 75121 at LRV-8 on Apollo 17 map (Meyer 1973). S73-24071* 

### **Introduction**

75120 was scooped from the lunar regolith at LRV – 8 (while driving at high speed). It is nothing more than a typical mare soil.

### **Petrography**

The maturity of 75121 is Is /FeO = 67 and the average grain size is 56 microns (Morris 1978, Graf 1993). The agglutinate count is very high (63%).

### **Chemistry**

For some reason or other Korotev and Kremser (1992) decided to analyze it (figures 2 and 5).

Moore et al. (1974) determined 145 ppm carbon (figure 4) and Gibson and Moore (1974) reported 1140 ppm sulfur.
