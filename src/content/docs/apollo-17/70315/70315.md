## **Introduction**

70315 is a coarse-grained, vesicular basalt that has been rounded on all sides by micrometeorite bombardment (figure 1). It has not been well studied and no age is available.

## **Petrography**

Neal and Taylor (1993) give the only description of 70315, although a mineral mode is found in Brown et al. (1975). Large pyroxene grains poikilitically enclose plagioclase and ilmenite (figure 2).

## **Chemistry**
