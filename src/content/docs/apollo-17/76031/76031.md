181 grams

## **Introduction**

76030 was collected downslope from the big boulder at station 6, Apollo 16 (figure 1). It had increased FeO and Sc content indicating a large proportion of mare material.

# **Petrography**

Morris (1978) determined the maturity index (Is /FeO =64).

Meyer (1973) found that 76034 included several interesting anorthosite particles.

76035 is a large piece of impact melt rock described in Meyer (1994).
