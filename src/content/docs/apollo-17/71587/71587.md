### **Petrography**

71587 is an olivine-microporphyritic ilmenite basalt similar to 71525 (Warner et al. 1978). It has small *Figure 2: Map of station 1 showing location of* olivine and elongate ilmenite phenocrysts set in *rake samples.* variolitic to granular groundmass of pyroxene, plagioclase and ilmenite.
