#### **Introduction**

78135 is a medium-grained ilmenite basalt collected from the regolith at station 8, Apollo 17.

Gibson et al. (1976) reported 1895 ppm S.

#### **Cosmogenic isotopes and exposure ages**

Drozd et al. (1977) determined the cosmic ray exposure age to be 126 m.y.

Keith et al. (1974) determined the cosmic-ray-induced activity of  $^{22}$ Na = 74 dpm/kg,  $^{26}$ Al = 42 dpm/kg,  $^{46}$ Sc =

76 dpm/kg,  $^{48}V$  = 18 dpm/kg,  $^{54}Mn$  = 180 dpm/kg and  $^{56}Co$  = 240 dpm/kg.

### **Processing**

Chipped, not sawn. Only three thin sections.
