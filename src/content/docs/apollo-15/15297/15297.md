# **15297** – 34.9 grams Regolith Breccia

![](_page_0_Picture_1.jpeg)

*Figure 1 a,b,c,d: Fragments of 15279. Cube is 1 cm.* 

## **Introduction**

15297 is a collection of regolith breccias from the residue of sample container bag #3 – which also contained large breccia samples 15298 and 15299. They have not been studied.

## **Petrography**

Note: the classic studies of Apollo 15 regolith breccias by McKay et al. (1989) and Simon et al. (1986) did not include 15297.

## **Processing**

There are no thin sections of 15297.
