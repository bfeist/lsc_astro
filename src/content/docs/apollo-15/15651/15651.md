#### **Introduction**

The large rake sample from station 9a, Apollo 15 included 15651 (figure 1). It is a medium-grained olivine-bearing mare basalt with microgabbroic texture (figure 2). In places the texture has been somewhat cataclasized by shock.

#### **Petrography**

Dowty et al. (1973) reported the mineral mode and determined the composition of the major minerals (figure 3). Pyroxene and olivine are chemically zoned and similar to that of olivine-normative basalts. Opaque minerals occur in clumps (Nehru et al. 1974).

#### **Chemistry**

Ryder and Steele (1981) determined the composition, finding that 15651 was an olivine-normative basalt (although silica was a bit high).
