# **15025**

## Regolith Breccia 77.3 grams

![](_page_0_Picture_2.jpeg)

*Figure 1a: Photo of 15025. Sample is 4 cm across. S71-44871* 

![](_page_0_Picture_4.jpeg)

15025 was included in the collection of the contingency sample for Apollo 15. It is a typical soil breccia.

### **Petrography**

McKay et al. (1989) reported that the maturity index for 15025 was Is /FeO = 30. It has clasts of mare basalt and KREEP basalt, as well as abundant glass.

See also soil sample 15020.

### **Chemistry**

15025 has higher trace element content than Apollo 15 soils (figure 5). Best and Minkin (1972) studied the composition of glass in 15025.
