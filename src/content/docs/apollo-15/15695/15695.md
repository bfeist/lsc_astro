## **15695, 15696 and 15697**

Vesicular Mare Basalt 10.67, 12.83 and 4.13 grams

![](_page_0_Picture_2.jpeg)

*Figure 1: Photo of 15695. Scale in cm/mm. S75- 33962.* 

## **Introduction**

These three chips of mare basalt were split out of the station 9a rake sample in 1975 and have not been studied. There are no thin sections. See section of 15614 for probable description.
