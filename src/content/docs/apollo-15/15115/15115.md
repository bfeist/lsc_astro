## **15115**

## Pigeonite Basalt 4 grams

## **Introduction**

15115 is a relatively coarse basalt. It was collected as a rake sample from station 2, Apollo 15.

Gose et al. (1972) and Pearce et al. (1973) determined the magnetic properties and Ma et al. (1978) reported the chemical composition (see figures).
